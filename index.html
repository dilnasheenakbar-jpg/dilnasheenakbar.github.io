Project 01
Lead Follow-up Automation for a Local Clinic
Healthcare · Small Business
The Problem

A clinic was losing new patient enquiries because nobody was following up quickly. Receptionist was manually sending emails one by one — taking 2+ hours daily.


What I Built

An n8n workflow that captures new enquiries from their website form, saves to a Google Sheet, and immediately sends a personalised welcome email and WhatsApp message — all without anyone touching it.

Project 02
WhatsApp Bot for Online Store Owner
E-commerce · Solo Entrepreneur
The Problem

A solo store owner was spending 4+ hours every day answering the same WhatsApp questions — "What's the price?", "Is this in stock?", "How do I order?" — taking time away from growing the business.


What I Built

A simple AI-powered WhatsApp bot using n8n and OpenAI that answers product questions, sends price lists, and collects order requests automatically. Escalates to the owner only for new or complex queries.

70%

4 hrs

Project 03
Social Media Auto-Posting for a Content Creator
Content Creation · Freelancer
The Problem

A content creator was spending too much time manually posting the same content across Instagram and Facebook. Inconsistent posting was hurting their reach.


What I Built

A Notion-to-social automation: the creator writes content once in Notion with a schedule date, and n8n automatically publishes to Instagram and Facebook at the right time — including caption, image, and hashtags.
