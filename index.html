Lead Follow-up Automation for a Local Clinic
Healthcare · Small Business
The Problem

A clinic was losing new patient enquiries because nobody was following up quickly. Receptionist was manually sending emails one by one — taking 2+ hours daily.


What I Built

An automated workflow that captures new enquiries from their website form, saves to a Google Sheet, and immediately sends a personalised welcome email and WhatsApp message — all without anyone touching it.

Project 02
AI Customer Support Inbox for Online Store Owner
E-commerce · Solo Entrepreneur
The Problem

A solo store owner was spending 4+ hours every day answering the same customer questions — pricing, stock, and order status — taking time away from growing the business.


What I Built

An AI-powered support system that reads incoming messages, drafts replies, categorizes tickets by urgency, and escalates only the complex ones to the owner directly.
